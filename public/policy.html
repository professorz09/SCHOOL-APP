About this page. EduGrow is a school management platform used by Indian schools. Accounts are managed — each student, parent, teacher, and principal account is created and administered by their school. Individual users cannot create accounts on their own.

1. Privacy Policy

1.1 Information we collect

EduGrow stores only the information required to operate a school: full name, mobile number, role (Student, Parent, Teacher, Principal, Driver), school assignment, class/section, attendance, exam results, fees, transport routes, and notices. For optional features, the school may add date of birth, Aadhaar number (encrypted at rest), address, and parent contact details.

1.2 How we use it

  • To run the school's day-to-day operations inside the app (attendance, fees, exams, transport, notices).
  • To authenticate users via mobile number + password.
  • To generate reports and exports for the school's own use.
  • To send service notifications inside the app (birthday greetings, fee reminders).

1.3 What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.
  • We do not run third-party advertising inside the app.
  • We do not share Aadhaar, mobile, or contact details outside the school's own users.

1.4 Where data lives

Data is hosted on Supabase (Postgres) in a region chosen for India-based latency. Backups are encrypted. Access is restricted by Row-Level Security policies — a student cannot read another student's record, a teacher cannot read another class's data unless explicitly permitted by the principal.

1.5 Children

Student accounts are administered by their school. We do not solicit information directly from children. Parents/guardians who notice incorrect data should ask the school principal to correct it.

2. Terms of Service

2.1 Acceptable use

By using EduGrow you agree to use it only for its intended purpose — administering or participating in your school. You must not attempt to access other schools' data, bulk-export data, or impersonate other users.

2.2 Account responsibility

Keep your password confidential. Notify your school principal immediately if you suspect unauthorised access. The school principal can reset any account password.

2.3 Suspension

We may suspend access if a user violates these terms, if a school's payment is unsettled past a grace period, or if a court order or legitimate legal request requires it.

2.4 No warranty

EduGrow is provided "as is". We work hard to keep it reliable, but we do not guarantee zero downtime. Critical academic records (results, mark-sheets) should also be preserved by the school in physical or independent digital form.

3. Account & School Deletion

Important: EduGrow uses managed accounts. Students, parents, teachers, and drivers do not have a self-service "delete my account" button because their record is part of their school's roster. To remove a single user, contact the school principal. To remove an entire school (and every account inside it), the principal initiates the request.

3.1 Removing a single user (student / parent / teacher / driver)

  1. Contact your school principal in person, by phone, or via the app's Helpdesk feature.
  2. The principal removes the user from the school's roster through their Settings → Users panel.
  3. The removal takes effect immediately; the user can no longer log in.

3.2 Deleting an entire school

A school deletion is a guarded three-stage process so nothing is lost by mistake:

  1. Principal requests deletion from Settings → Danger Zone. The request travels to the platform admin.
  2. Platform admin reviews and either approves or denies. Approval simply unlocks the principal's "Delete" button — no data is touched yet.
  3. Principal confirms deletion by typing their mobile-number last four digits + the phrase DELETE MY SCHOOL. The school enters a 30-day "soft-deleted" state where it is inaccessible but the data still exists.
  4. 30-day grace window: the platform admin can restore the school any time during this window — every user, every record returns intact.
  5. After 30 days, the platform admin may manually permanently delete the school. There is no automated job that destroys data. The action is irreversible and requires another typed-phrase confirmation.

3.3 Account deletion request URL

If you need to record an account deletion request for compliance purposes (for example, in your data-protection diary), use this page as the canonical URL. The mechanism is contact-based — message your school principal or the platform admin at the address in the Contact section below.

4. Data Retention

  • Active school: data is retained for as long as the school continues using EduGrow.
  • Single user removed: the user row is deleted; historical records (attendance entries, exam marks, fee payments) remain in the school's records because they are part of the school's official register.
  • Soft-deleted school: all data is preserved for 30 days for restore.
  • Permanently deleted school: all primary records are removed. Encrypted backups roll out within the next backup retention cycle (typically 30 days).
  • Audit logs: retained for one year then archived. They contain action metadata only (who changed what, when) — not message bodies or document contents.
